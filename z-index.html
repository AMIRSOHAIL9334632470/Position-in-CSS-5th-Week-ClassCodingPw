Physics Wallah Private Limited (also known as just Physics Wallah or PW) is an Indian educational technology company headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh. It was founded by Alakh Pandey and Prateek Maheshwari in 2020. It became a unicorn in June 2022 after raising $100 million at a valuation of $1.1 billion.
